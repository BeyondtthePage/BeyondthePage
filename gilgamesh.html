Gilgamesh’s Journey

Follow the major stages of Gilgamesh’s journey and open the relevant tablet guide.

🏛️ Uruk Gilgamesh rules the city and meets Enkidu. → 🌲 Cedar Forest The heroes confront Humbaba. → 🐂 Return to Uruk They defeat the Bull of Heaven. → 🏜️ The Wilderness Gilgamesh wanders after Enkidu’s death. → ⛰️ Mount Mashu He crosses the dark path of the sun. → 🍷 Siduri’s Tavern Siduri directs him toward Ur-shanabi. → 🌊 Waters of Death Ur-shanabi guides him across the deadly waters. → 🌿 Uta-napishti Gilgamesh learns why immortality is denied. → 🧱 Uruk Again He returns to the city and its lasting walls.
